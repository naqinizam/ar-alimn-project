WHAT IS AR?

Technology that overlays digital content (like 3D models) onto the real world through devices like smartphones or tablets.

HOW IT WORKS?

  1. Allow camera access when prompted.
  2. Point your rear camera at a flat surface.
  3. A chair will appear in your space!
